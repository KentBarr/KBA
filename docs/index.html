About Me

They tell me I'm from Manitoba. But then, they could have told me anything.

I composed and shot my first photograph before I could talk. I took it with a Kodak Brownie Hawkeye which, because of the primitive optics, presents the image with left and right reversed as in a mirror. This was maddening to a toddler. Not only that, once I had managed to fiddle that out, I very carefully composed the shot so my parents' heads were cleanly in the frame. Then the photos came back from the drugstore and the very tops of Dad's head was cut off. Since that day I have hated all camera viewfinders *except* SLR's that show exactly 100% of the frame. ALL of them, even Leicas which are of course very impressive and beautiful machines. But fuck 'em. Because paralax.
